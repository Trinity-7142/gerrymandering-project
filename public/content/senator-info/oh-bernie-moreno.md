Bernie Moreno is a Republican serving as the senior U.S. Senator from Ohio. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, he immigrated with his family to Fort Lauderdale, Florida at age five. He purchased his first car dealership in 2005 and grew it into one of the largest dealership groups in America, eventually employing over 1,000 Ohioans. He later pivoted to blockchain technology before it received mainstream attention. A Trump-aligned conservative, his platform included stricter immigration policies and completing the border wall. The crypto industry spent more than $40 million supporting his 2024 campaign. He is the first Hispanic American senator from Ohio and one of the first two Colombian American U.S. senators. Before his Senate run, Moreno settled over a dozen wage theft lawsuits and was ordered to pay over $400,000 to two former employees.
