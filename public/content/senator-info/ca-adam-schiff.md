Adam Schiff is the junior U.S. Senator from California, a seat he has held since 2024. Born in Massachusetts, Schiff settled in Alamo, California, with his father, a clothing salesman. He worked summers in his father's lumber yard and as a seasonal firefighter to help pay for school, later becoming a federal prosecutor who successfully prosecuted a crooked FBI agent turned Russian spy. Starting as a member of the centrist Blue Dog Coalition, he has rebranded as a progressive, vowing to improve housing affordability, protect labor rights, and provide undocumented immigrants a path to citizenship. His platform centers on affordable housing, defending democracy, lowering health care costs, and protecting the environment. He served as Lead Impeachment Manager during the first Senate impeachment trial of President Donald Trump. He was censured by the House in June 2023 for "misleading the American public."
