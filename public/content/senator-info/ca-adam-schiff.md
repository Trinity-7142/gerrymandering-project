Sen. Schiff serves as the junior United States Senator from California
, representing the state at large. 
Born in Framingham, Massachusetts, his father was a clothing salesman, and the family moved to Alamo, California in pursuit of the California dream.
 
Initially a member of the centrist Blue Dog Coalition, Schiff has rebranded himself as a progressive Democrat
, with 
an academic background from Stanford University and Harvard Law School.
 
He campaigned pledging to take on the affordable housing crisis and bring down health care costs,
 while also prioritizing 
ending federal subsidies for oil and gas production and addressing climate-related challenges.
 
He served as Lead Impeachment Manager during the first Senate impeachment trial of President Donald Trump,
 and 
his earlier legislative work on public transit expansion earned him the nickname "Father of the Gold Line."
 
In June 2023, Schiff was censured by the U.S. House of Representatives for "misleading the American public and for conduct unbecoming of an elected Member."
