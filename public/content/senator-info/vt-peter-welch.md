Peter Welch is an American lawyer and politician serving as the junior United States Senator from Vermont. Born in Springfield, Massachusetts, to a dentist father and homemaker mother, he settled in White River Junction, Vermont after law school, becoming one of the county's first public defenders for low-income residents. A progressive Democrat, his Senate priorities include lowering prescription drug costs, combating climate change, expanding rural broadband, and protecting democracy. Elected at age 75, he became the oldest person ever to enter the Senate as a freshman. He also gained national attention in July 2024 as the first Democratic senator to publicly call on President Biden to withdraw from the presidential race.
