Michael Dean Crapo is the senior United States Senator from Idaho, a seat he has held since 1999. Born in Idaho Falls, he graduated from Brigham Young University and Harvard Law School, practicing law in his home city throughout the 1980s. Considered politically conservative, he holds a lifetime conservative rating of 91.30 from the American Conservative Union. He strongly supports free market policies, with extending the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, expanding affordable healthcare, and promoting free and fair trade among his top priorities. He became Chairman of the Senate Finance Committee in January 2025. He has also demonstrated a willingness to forge bipartisan alliances, notably as a member of the "Gang of Six," which worked to mediate debate over the national debt in 2011.
