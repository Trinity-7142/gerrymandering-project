Jerry Moran is the senior United States Senator from Kansas, a seat he has held since 2011. Born in Great Bend, Kansas, he was raised in Plainville. He studied economics at the University of Kansas and later earned his J.D. from the University of Kansas School of Law, following an early career as a bank officer. A conservative Republican, his platform centers on fiscal restraint, agricultural support, and veterans' care. He has opposed reckless spending and pushed for tougher funding standards and broad reform. As chairman of the Senate Veterans' Affairs Committee, he championed the PACT Act, expanding benefits for veterans exposed to burn pits. He also chaired the NRSC, overseeing Republicans' gain of nine Senate seats in 2014.
