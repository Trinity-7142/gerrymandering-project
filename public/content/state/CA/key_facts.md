**Content from Claude Opus 4.6**: *California* uses an **independent citizens redistricting commission**, established by Proposition 11 (2008) and Proposition 20 (2010), to draw both congressional and state legislative maps.

The commission is composed of **14 members** — 5 Democrats, 5 Republicans, and 4 from neither major party — selected through an independent application and screening process.

California's **52 congressional districts** span one of the most geographically and demographically diverse states in the nation, from rural agricultural valleys to dense coastal metros.

The current maps were drawn in **2022** following the 2020 Census and took effect for the 2022 midterm elections.
