Aaron Bean (R) has served as U.S. Representative for Florida's 4th congressional district since 2023. The grandson of a coal miner and son of teachers, Bean grew up as the baby of nine children in a working-class family in his hometown of Fernandina Beach, Florida. Prior to Congress, he served as a community bank president and built four small businesses. A conservative Republican, his platform centers on fiscal restraint, school choice, and healthcare reform. Now in his second term and serving on the Ways and Means Committee, Bean founded and co-chairs the Delivering Outstanding Government Efficiency (DOGE) Caucus, aimed at reducing government fraud, waste, and abuse. He has introduced legislation including the HSA's For All Act and the Punishing Health Care Fraudsters Act. Bean has earned an "A+" rating on the Susan B. Anthony List's National Pro-Life Scorecard.
