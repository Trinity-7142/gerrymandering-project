Born in Fountain County, Indiana, in 1945, Baird resides outside of Greencastle, Indiana. He earned his bachelor's and master's degrees in animal science from Purdue University and his doctorate in monogastric nutrition from the University of Kentucky. He is the owner of Baird Family Farms, Triple Tree Enterprises, Inc., and Indiana Home Care Plus of Greencastle, a home health agency. A conservative Republican, his platform emphasizes economic growth, American agriculture, military strength, border security, and advancing a "America First" agenda. He sits on the House committees on Foreign Affairs, Science, Space, and Technology, and Agriculture. Baird is one of three deployed Vietnam War veterans currently serving in the House; he lost his left arm during a convoy ambush in Vietnam and earned a Bronze Star and two Purple Hearts. Notably, Baird was ranked the Republican Member of Congress with the highest staff turnover in 2023, second most overall in the House, per LegiStorm's Worst Bosses Index.
