Ralph Norman is a Republican politician and real estate developer who has served as the U.S. Representative for South Carolina's 5th congressional district since 2017. Born and raised in Rock Hill, South Carolina, he resides there today and runs the Warren Norman Company, a real estate firm founded by his father. GovTrack ranked Norman the most conservative member of the 117th Congress as of February 2023. He is a member of the House Freedom Caucus. Throughout his career, he has consistently voted in favor of limited government and individual liberties. He voted against the Fiscal Responsibility Act of 2023, advocating for deeper federal spending cuts, and co-sponsored legislation to require a balanced federal budget. Notably, Norman endorsed longtime ally Nikki Haley for president in the 2024 Republican primaries, making him the only member of Congress to do so. In July 2025, Norman announced he was running for Governor of South Carolina in the 2026 election.
