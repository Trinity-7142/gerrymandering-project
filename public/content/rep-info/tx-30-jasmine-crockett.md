Rep. Jasmine Crockett (D-TX) has served as the U.S. Representative for Texas's 30th Congressional District since 2023, having previously represented the 100th district in the Texas House of Representatives. Born in St. Louis, Missouri, she graduated from Rhodes College and earned her J.D. from the University of Houston Law Center. She began her legal career as a public defender in Bowie County, Texas, and later ran a law firm representing victims of alleged police brutality. She has been labeled a progressive Democrat, though she has personally distanced herself from that label, calling her positions "common sense." Her platform centers on criminal justice reform, voting rights, economic equity, and healthcare access. In the 119th Congress, she was appointed Vice Ranking Member of the House Committee on Oversight and Government Reform and Ranking Member of the House Judiciary Subcommittee on Oversight. She gained national attention after a highly publicized exchange with Rep. Marjorie Taylor Greene during a 2024 House Oversight Committee meeting, which went viral and was spoofed on *Saturday Night Live*. In 2026, she introduced the Payback Act, a bill aimed at refunding Americans for extra costs incurred due to certain Trump-era tariffs.
