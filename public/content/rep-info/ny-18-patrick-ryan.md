Patrick Ryan is an American businessman, politician, and former U.S. Army intelligence officer representing New York's Hudson Valley. Born and raised in Kingston, NY, his mother was a public school teacher and his father owned a small business. He earned a B.S. in international politics from West Point and a master's in security studies from Georgetown University. Ryan served two combat tours in Iraq, earning two Bronze Stars. A moderate Democrat and member of the New Democrat Coalition, his platform centers on affordable health care, preserving Social Security and Medicare, gun violence prevention, and reproductive rights. He serves on the House Armed Services and Transportation and Infrastructure Committees. He is the first West Point graduate to represent the Academy in the U.S. House. Notably, in July 2024, Ryan called for Joe Biden to withdraw from the presidential race.
