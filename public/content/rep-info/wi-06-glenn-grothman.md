Glenn Grothman is an American attorney and politician serving as the U.S. Representative from Wisconsin's 6th congressional district. He hails from Glenbeulah, Wisconsin, and earned a BBA from the University of Wisconsin–Madison in 1978 and a J.D. from its law school in 1983, subsequently practicing law in West Bend. First elected to Congress in 2014, he previously served 21 years in the Wisconsin Legislature. A staunch conservative, he sponsors legislation primarily in education, taxation, health, and labor. His platform focuses on skills-based education, eliminating marriage penalties in government assistance programs, expanding generic drug access, and restoring election integrity. Axios reported that Grothman had a "Trump Loyalty Index" of 93 in 2020, higher than any other member of Congress. Notably, in 2024 he voted against the $60 billion military aid package for Ukraine.
