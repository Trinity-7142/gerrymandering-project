Sheri Biggs (R) is a first-term U.S. Representative from South Carolina's 3rd Congressional District. Originally from Kosciusko, Mississippi, she brings over three decades of healthcare experience, from ICU nursing to mental health consultation for veterans and first responders. She served in the Air National Guard, reaching the rank of lieutenant colonel. She campaigns as a pro-life, pro-Second Amendment conservative Republican. She is only the third woman from South Carolina elected to the House and the first to represent the Third District. In Congress, she serves on the Homeland Security; Foreign Affairs; and Science, Space, and Technology Committees. Legislative priorities include strengthening mental health support for military families and the Defending Women in the Workplace Act, aimed at protecting women's privacy and safety in the workplace. She has missed zero of 486 roll call votes since taking office in January 2025.
