Christopher Raphael Deluzio is a progressive Democrat serving as U.S. Representative for Pennsylvania's 17th congressional district since 2023. Born in Pittsburgh and raised in Thornburg, he is an Iraq War veteran, voting rights attorney, and union organizer. He earned a B.S. from the U.S. Naval Academy in 2006 and a J.D. magna cum laude from Georgetown Law in 2013. He focuses on strengthening labor protections, expanding healthcare access, and safeguarding democracy. Deluzio supports universal healthcare and co-sponsored the Medicare for All Act, as well as the PRO Act expanding workers' rights to unionize. He co-sponsored the bipartisan Railway Safety Act of 2023 following the East Palestine train derailment. He won re-election in 2024, defeating Republican Rob Mercuri 54% to 46%.
