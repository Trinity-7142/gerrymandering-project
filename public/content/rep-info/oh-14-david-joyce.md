David Patrick Joyce is a Republican attorney serving Ohio's 14th Congressional District since 2013, representing Northeast Ohio counties including Ashtabula, Geauga, Lake, and Trumbull. Born in Cleveland to an Irish Catholic family whose father was a coal salesman, Joyce worked as a public defender before being elected Geauga County Prosecutor in 1988, serving nearly 25 years. He chairs the Republican Governance Group, a caucus of moderate Republicans. His platform centers on fiscal responsibility, reducing inflation, cutting red tape, and supporting jobs-training programs. He has been a fierce advocate for the Great Lakes, securing annual funding for the Great Lakes Restoration Initiative. Joyce has also introduced bipartisan cannabis reform legislation, including a bill to remove cannabis from the Controlled Substances Act. The Lugar Center ranked him the most bipartisan House member from Ohio during the 114th Congress.
