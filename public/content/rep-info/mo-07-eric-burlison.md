Born in Springfield, Missouri, Eric Burlison is a sixth-generation Missourian with 20 years of private sector experience as an investment advisor and software consultant. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy and a Master of Business Administration from Missouri State University. A member of the House Freedom Caucus, Burlison is a staunch conservative who champions Second Amendment rights, fiscal restraint, and social conservatism. He introduced the Great American Healthcare Plan, a comprehensive reform package centered on transparency, competition, and individual choice. He is a member of the House DOGE Committee and sponsored the Smart Space Act of 2026 to address taxpayer dollars spent on underused federal office space. Notably, Burlison was one of only two representatives to vote against the Take It Down Act, targeting non-consensual intimate imagery, without elaborating on his opposition.
