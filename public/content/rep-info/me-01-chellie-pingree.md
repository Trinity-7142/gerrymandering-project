Chellie Pingree is a Democratic U.S. Representative for Maine's 1st congressional district, serving since 2009. A self-described back-to-the-lander and organic farmer, she settled on a Maine island after college, where she and her husband started a small farm. She is the first Democratic woman elected to the U.S. House of Representatives from Maine. A progressive voice, she has urged a national climate emergency declaration and supports the Green New Deal. Her legislative focus falls heavily on agriculture and food, public lands, and immigration. As a state legislator, she authored the nation's first bill regulating prescription drug prices, Maine Rx. She currently serves on both the House Agriculture and Appropriations Committees. She is vice chair of the Congressional Progressive Caucus.
