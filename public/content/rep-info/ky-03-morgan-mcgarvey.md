Morgan McGarvey is an attorney and politician serving as the U.S. Representative for Kentucky's 3rd congressional district since 2023. Born and raised in Louisville, he earned a bachelor's degree in journalism from the University of Missouri and a J.D. from the University of Kentucky College of Law. A Democrat, he is currently the only Democrat in Kentucky's congressional delegation. Before Congress, he served a decade in the Kentucky State Senate, including four years as Minority Leader, passing 18 bills into law. In 2018, he became one of the youngest members of a state legislature to serve in a leadership role. His platform centers on healthcare access and strong public education. In a Republican-controlled Congress, he has claimed over $12 million in federal funding and $245 million in federal grants for Louisville. Notably, McGarvey missed only 0.3% of roll call votes, better than the median among currently serving representatives.
