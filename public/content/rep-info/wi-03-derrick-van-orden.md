Derrick Van Orden is a Republican politician and retired U.S. Navy SEAL representing Wisconsin's 3rd congressional district since 2023. He and his brother were raised in rural poverty in Minnesota, retiring after 26 years as a Navy SEAL Senior Chief with multiple combat tours. Van Orden focused his 2022 campaign on his military service and the economy, and has emphasized providing for farmers, veterans, and servicemembers, and fiscal responsibility. He serves on the House Agriculture, Armed Services, and Veterans' Affairs committees. Notable controversies include his attendance at the January 6 "Stop the Steal" rally and presence at the Capitol during the attack, and a 2021 citation for carrying a loaded handgun through airport security, for which he paid a fine and completed a gun safety program.
