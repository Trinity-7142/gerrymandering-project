Erin Houchin is a Republican U.S. Representative for Indiana's 9th Congressional District, serving since 2023. A native of Southern Indiana, she is from Salem. She is a small business owner who operated a communications firm focused on helping law enforcement. She previously represented the 47th District in the Indiana State Senate for eight years. A strong conservative, her platform centers on protecting the unborn, defending the Second Amendment, cutting taxes, and expanding rural broadband. She serves on the House Rules, Budget, and Energy & Commerce Committees. Following the 2024 elections, Houchin won the House Republican Conference election for secretary, the sixth-highest-ranking post in the conference. She has also advanced bipartisan bills protecting children online.
