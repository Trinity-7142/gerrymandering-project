Born in Tucson and raised in Yuma, Arizona, Eli Crane is a Republican politician, businessman, and former U.S. Navy SEAL serving as the representative for Arizona's 2nd congressional district since 2023. He completed five wartime deployments, three to Iraq with SEAL Team 3, serving for 13 years. Before politics, he co-founded Bottle Breacher, a company producing patriotic-themed bottle openers made from used bullets that attracted investment on a 2014 episode of *Shark Tank*. Crane is a member of the House Freedom Caucus and campaigns on a pro-life, pro-Second Amendment platform. His legislative priorities include border security and combating fentanyl trafficking. Notable actions include passing the North Rim Restoration Act of 2025 to expedite the rebuild of the Grand Canyon's North Rim, and introducing articles of impeachment against a federal judge in 2025 over DOGE access to Treasury records. He won re-election in 2024 against Democratic nominee Jonathan Nez, the former Navajo Nation president.
