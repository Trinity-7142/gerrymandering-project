Joaquin Castro is a Democratic lawyer and politician who has represented Texas's 20th congressional district since 2013. Born and raised in San Antonio, he is the son of a retired mathematics teacher and a community activist. He graduated with honors from Stanford University and earned his J.D. from Harvard Law School. His platform centers on an "Infrastructure of Opportunity" — public schools, healthcare, and good-paying jobs — to help Americans pursue upward mobility. He serves on the House Foreign Affairs Committee and the House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence. He was recognized as the most effective Democratic House member in the 118th Congress by the Center for Effective Lawmaking. Notable community initiatives include the Trailblazers College Tour for underprivileged students and SA READS, San Antonio's largest literacy campaign.
