Donald John Bacon is a Republican politician and retired military officer representing Nebraska's 2nd congressional district since 2017. Raised on a farm in the Midwest, Bacon learned the value of hard work and self-reliance. He commanded the 55th Wing at Offutt Air Force Base and retired at the rank of Brigadier General. He lists Papillion, Nebraska as his hometown. A center-right conservative, he champions free-market economic principles, working to ease the cost of living, cut taxes, and reduce the national debt. The Center for Effective Lawmaking identified him as the most effective Republican lawmaker in the House during the 117th Congress. He has won his Omaha-based district even in years when its voters backed the Democratic presidential candidate. In June 2025, Bacon announced he would not seek re-election in 2026.
