Raised by a single mother in a low-income Southern California neighborhood, Anna Paulina Luna is a Republican Air Force veteran serving as U.S. Representative for Florida's 13th congressional district and the first Mexican-American woman elected to Congress from Florida. She joined the U.S. Air Force at 19 and later earned a biology degree from the University of West Florida. Before her election, she also served as National Hispanic Outreach Director for Turning Point USA. A staunch conservative, her platform emphasizes border security, opposing illegal immigration, and finishing the border wall. She introduced the End Congressional Stock Trading Act, which would ban members of Congress and their families from trading individual stocks. Notably, she sponsored a resolution censuring Rep. Adam Schiff, which passed 213–209. She voted against the $60 billion military aid package for Ukraine in April 2024.
