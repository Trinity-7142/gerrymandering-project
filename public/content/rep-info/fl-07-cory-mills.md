Cory Mills is a Republican politician, businessman, and Army veteran serving as the U.S. representative for Florida's 7th congressional district since 2023. Born in Winter Haven and raised in Auburndale, Florida, Mills was raised by his grandparents. He received the Bronze Star while serving as a U.S. Army sniper in Iraq and later co-founded PACEM Solutions International. He serves on the House Foreign Affairs and Armed Services Committees. His platform centers on national security, law enforcement support, and America First conservatism. Notably, he helped evacuate over 255 Americans following the October 7, 2023 attacks in Israel. The House Ethics Committee announced an investigation into Mills in 2025 for allegedly violating federal laws prohibiting members of Congress from contracting with the government. A Florida judge also granted a restraining order against Mills in October 2025 stemming from a separate personal legal matter.
