Rep. Sylvia Garcia (D) has represented Texas's 29th congressional district since 2019. Born in San Diego, Texas, and raised in Palito Blanco, she is the eighth of ten children in a Mexican American family. She earned a scholarship to Texas Woman's University and later worked multiple jobs to fund her J.D. from Thurgood Marshall School of Law at Texas Southern University. A progressive Democrat, Garcia advocates for fair immigration policies and, as lead sponsor of the Dream and Promise Act, has championed a pathway to citizenship for Dreamers. She supports the Women's Health Protection Act to safeguard access to reproductive healthcare. She became the first Hispanic member of the Houston Congressional Delegation and one of the first two Latinas to represent Texas in Congress. In January 2020, Garcia was selected as one of seven House impeachment managers who presented the impeachment case against President Donald Trump before the Senate. She currently serves as Vice Ranking Member of the House Financial Services Committee.
