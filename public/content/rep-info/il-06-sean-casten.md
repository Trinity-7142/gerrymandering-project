Sean Casten is a Democratic businessman and politician serving as the U.S. Representative for Illinois's 6th congressional district. Born in Dublin, Ireland, to American parents and raised in Hartsdale, New York, he earned a B.A. in molecular biology and biochemistry from Middlebury College. He later co-founded Recycled Energy Development and served as president and CEO of Turbosteam Corporation, a clean energy manufacturing firm. He now lives in Downers Grove, Illinois. A progressive Democrat, he has championed legislation lowering costs, creating jobs, affirming women's rights, protecting seniors, and advocating for clean energy. He was a key author of the Inflation Reduction Act, the largest investment in climate action in American history. Notably, in July 2024, Casten publicly called on President Biden to withdraw from the presidential race.
