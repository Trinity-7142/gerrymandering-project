Sharice Davids is a Democratic attorney and former mixed martial artist representing Kansas's 3rd Congressional District, which covers most of the Kansas side of the Kansas City metropolitan area. Raised by a single mother who served in the U.S. Army, she was the first in her family to attend college, working multiple jobs to put herself through school and ultimately earning a law degree from Cornell. She is the first openly LGBT Native American elected to Congress and one of the first two Native American women elected to the House. Her platform centers on lowering the cost of living, bringing good-paying jobs to the Kansas City region, and making government work for people over special interests. In 2024, Davids reached her highest-ever bipartisanship record, with 83 percent of the bills she supported having bipartisan backing. Since being sworn in, she has helped return more than $21 million to constituents.
