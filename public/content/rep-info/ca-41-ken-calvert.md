Born and raised in Corona, California, Ken Calvert is a lifelong Riverside County resident and 17-year small business owner with experience in the restaurant and real estate industries. He has served as the U.S. Representative for California's 41st congressional district as a Republican since 1993, making him the Dean of the California Republican Delegation. His platform centers on pro-growth economic policy, strong national defense, and border security. He currently chairs the House Defense Appropriations Subcommittee. Calvert is the original author of the E-Verify law, and voted for the Respect for Marriage Act in 2022. Notably, he voted to reject the certification of Arizona's and Pennsylvania's electoral votes in January 2021.
