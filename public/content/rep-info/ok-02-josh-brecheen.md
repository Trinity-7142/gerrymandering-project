Josh Brecheen is a Republican U.S. Representative for Oklahoma's 2nd congressional district, serving since 2023. He hails from Coalgate, Oklahoma. A fourth-generation rancher, he describes himself as a committed Christian who has also operated a small heavy equipment and trucking business. He is a citizen of the Choctaw Nation. He holds a dual degree in animal science and agricultural communications from Oklahoma State University. He previously served in the Oklahoma State Senate from 2010 to 2018. His platform centers on energy dominance, traditional values, border security, constitutional adherence, and reducing deficit spending. He advocates for congressional term limits as the key solution to Washington corruption, a pledge he honored during his state senate tenure. Notably, he introduced the Patriotism Not Pride Act, which would bar federal funds for Pride Month events and prohibit federal agencies from displaying the Pride flag. In 2024, he voted against the $60 billion military aid package for Ukraine. GovTrack ranked him among the 12th most politically conservative House freshmen.
