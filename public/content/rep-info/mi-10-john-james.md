John James is a Republican politician, businessman, and former military officer serving as the U.S. Representative for Michigan's 10th congressional district since 2023. Born in Southfield and raised in the Palmer Woods neighborhood of Detroit, he graduated from West Point in 2004 and served eight years in the Army, completing multiple tours in Operation Iraqi Freedom as an AH-64 Apache pilot. He later joined his family's firm, James Group International, eventually becoming president of the company and CEO of its subsidiary, Renaissance Global Logistics. Affordability is a top stated priority; he has committed to tackling rising housing costs, lowering the tax burden, and addressing healthcare expenses. He has described himself as "a pro-life, pro-second amendment, pro-business conservative." He serves on the House Committee on Energy and Commerce. In April 2025, James announced his candidacy for governor of Michigan in the 2026 election.
