Cliff Bentz is a Republican attorney serving as U.S. Representative for Oregon's 2nd congressional district since 2021. He was born in Salem and raised on ranches in the eastern Oregon communities of Fields and Drewsey. He has been a law partner in Ontario, Oregon since 1980, specializing in agricultural, water, and real property law. OR-02 is a largely rural district covering about two-thirds of eastern Oregon. Bentz focuses on advocating for agriculture, water resources, and a balanced approach to natural resource conservation. He sponsors bills primarily in public lands, natural resources, and environmental protection. He previously served in both chambers of the Oregon legislature. A 2025 investigation found Bentz used federal funds for "luxury" accommodations while traveling. He also drew attention for objecting to Pennsylvania's electoral votes following the 2020 election.
