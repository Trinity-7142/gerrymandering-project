Paul Gosar is a Republican politician and former dentist serving as the U.S. Representative for Arizona's 9th congressional district. Raised in Pinedale, Wyoming, his career experience prior to Congress includes working as a dentist. From 1989 to 2010, Gosar practiced dentistry in Flagstaff, Arizona, was named the Arizona Dental Association's "Dentist of the Year" in 2001, and was inducted into the AzDA Hall of Fame. He is a member of the House Freedom Caucus and is considered a far-right politician who opposes the Affordable Care Act, abortion, gun control, and immigration. He has sponsored legislation to promote local control of land use and has served on the House Committee on Natural Resources. In November 2021, Gosar was formally censured by the U.S. House of Representatives and stripped of his committee assignments after he posted an anime video on social media depicting violence against Representative Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and President Joe Biden. After Republicans regained the House majority in 2022, Gosar was reinstated to his committee assignments.
