Whitesides's official hometown is Agua Dulce, California. He is a Democrat serving as U.S. Representative for California's 27th congressional district since 2025. He previously served as Chief of Staff at NASA and as CEO of Virgin Galactic. His campaign platform centered on creating local jobs, lowering daily costs, protecting reproductive freedom, defending Social Security and Medicare, and addressing the wildfire insurance crisis. Whitesides narrowly defeated incumbent Republican Mike Garcia in November 2024 by roughly 8,000 votes. In Congress, he serves on the House Armed Services Committee and the Science, Space, and Technology Committee. He was the only Democratic member selected to serve on three HASC subcommittees. He co-sponsored the bipartisan Fix Our Forests Act and has been a prominent advocate on wildfire prevention, having co-founded a wildfire prevention organization prior to taking office.
