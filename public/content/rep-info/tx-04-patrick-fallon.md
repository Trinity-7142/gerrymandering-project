Rep. Pat Fallon was born in Pittsfield, Massachusetts, to two public school teachers. He earned a B.A. in government and international relations from the University of Notre Dame, where he was part of the 1988 national championship football team. He served as an active-duty U.S. Air Force officer for four years, receiving an honorable discharge at the rank of captain. He is the president and CEO of Virtus Apparel, a Prosper, Texas-based company specializing in military and patriotic clothing with roughly 100 employees. A Republican, he has represented TX-04 since 2021, having previously served in both the Texas House and Senate. A staunch conservative, Fallon sponsors bills primarily focused on government operations, national security, and immigration. He is a member of the House Department of Government Efficiency Committee. In 2024, he was named to a bipartisan task force investigating the attempted assassination of Donald Trump.
