Clifford Scott Franklin (born August 23, 1964) is a conservative Republican, U.S. Navy veteran, and businessman raised in Lakeland, Florida. He earned a B.S. from the United States Naval Academy in 1986 and served as a Naval Aviator flying the S-3 Viking off multiple aircraft carriers. He later led Lakeland-based insurance firm Lanier Upshaw as President & CEO for 20 years. He represented Florida's 15th congressional district from 2021 to 2023 and has represented the 18th district since 2023. He serves on the House Appropriations Committee, with assignments on the Subcommittees for Agriculture, Military Construction and Veterans Affairs, and Energy and Water Development. His platform has centered on border security, Second Amendment rights, and opposition to abortion. From January 2021 through April 2026, Franklin missed just 1.1% of roll call votes, better than the median among currently serving representatives.
