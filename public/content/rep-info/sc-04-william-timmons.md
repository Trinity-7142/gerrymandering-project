A native of Greenville, South Carolina, William Richardson Timmons IV has served as the U.S. Representative for SC-04 since 2019. His background as a prosecutor and small business owner inspired him to seek public office. He holds a J.D. and a master's in international studies from the University of South Carolina, as well as an M.S. in Cybersecurity Risk and Strategy from NYU. A conservative Republican, he has cosponsored legislation to balance the budget, defund Planned Parenthood, strengthen national defense, and promote school choice. He sits on the House Financial Services Committee and the Oversight Subcommittee on Delivering on Government Efficiency. In December 2020, Timmons was one of 126 House Republicans to sign an amicus brief supporting Texas v. Pennsylvania, contesting the 2020 presidential election results. He also serves as a Captain and JAG officer in the South Carolina Air National Guard.
