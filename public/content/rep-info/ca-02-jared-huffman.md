Jared Huffman is an American lawyer and politician serving as the U.S. Representative for California's 2nd Congressional District since 2013. Born in Independence, Missouri, he graduated cum laude from Boston College Law School in 1990 and moved to the San Francisco Bay Area, where he worked as a Senior Attorney for the Natural Resources Defense Council and as a public interest attorney winning major gender and race discrimination cases. A progressive Democrat, he has pushed legislation to foster economic opportunity and protect the environmental and economic values of the North Coast, including rural and tribal communities. He now serves as Ranking Member on the Committee on Natural Resources in the 119th Congress. Huffman holds a lifetime environmental scorecard rating of 98% from the League of Conservation Voters. He is also co-founder and co-chair of the Congressional Freethought Caucus, which promotes public policy based on reason and science while protecting the secular character of government.
