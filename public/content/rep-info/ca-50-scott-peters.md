Scott Peters is a Democrat serving as the U.S. Representative for California's 50th congressional district. Born in Springfield, Ohio, and raised in Michigan, Peters grew up in a modest household — his father was a Lutheran minister, and he relied on student loans and work-study jobs to finance his education. He earned his undergraduate degree from Duke University and worked as an economist for the EPA before attending NYU School of Law. His district encompasses both coastal and central San Diego, along with the suburbs of Poway and Coronado. A centrist Democrat, Peters is a member of the New Democrat Coalition and the Problem Solvers Caucus. His career has been defined by legislative work on veterans' issues, clean energy, drug pricing, and the substance abuse crisis. He serves on the House Energy and Commerce and Budget Committees. Notably, in July 2024, Peters was among the Democratic members who publicly called on President Biden to withdraw from the presidential race.
