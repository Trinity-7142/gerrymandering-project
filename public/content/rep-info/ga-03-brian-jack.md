Brian Timothy Jack is a Republican politician and former political advisor serving as U.S. Representative for Georgia's 3rd congressional district since 2025. Born in Atlanta, he attended Woodward Academy in College Park, Georgia. After graduating from Pepperdine University, Jack worked for the RNC and AIPAC before joining Trump's 2016 presidential campaign. He served as White House Political Director from 2019 to 2021, then as a senior advisor on Trump's 2024 campaign. A close Trump ally, his platform emphasizes tax cuts, reduced spending, and expanding domestic energy production. He defeated Democrat Maura Keller with 66.3% of the vote. Since taking office, Jack has missed zero of 486 roll call votes. He is a sixth-generation Georgian.
