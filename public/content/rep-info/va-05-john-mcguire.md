John Joseph McGuire III is a politician, businessman, and former U.S. Navy SEAL born in Richmond, Virginia, who graduated from Henrico High School and served as a Navy SEAL from 1988 to 1998. Born and raised in Central Virginia, McGuire spent his early childhood in the foster care system, attending nine different elementary schools, before his grandparents rescued him and his sister and raised them. A conservative Republican, his platform focuses on limiting government, prioritizing safety, lowering living costs, and aligning with Trump's policies on immigration, security, and the economy. In the 119th Congress, he serves on the House Armed Services Committee and the House Committee on Oversight and Accountability. He won the 2024 Republican primary by defeating incumbent Bob Good by just 374 votes. Notable controversies include claiming the COVID-19 pandemic was designed to rig the 2020 election and admitting to attending the January 6, 2021 rally in Washington, D.C., while denying participation in the Capitol attack.
