Born in Houston and raised in the River Oaks neighborhood, Lizzie Fletcher is a Democrat and attorney who has represented Texas's 7th congressional district since 2019. She earned Phi Beta Kappa honors at Kenyon College and a law degree from William & Mary, before returning to Houston to work at the firm Vinson & Elkins. A moderate, she serves on the House Committee on Energy & Commerce and is a member of the pro-business New Democrat Coalition, where she chairs its Trade Task Force. Upon her swearing-in, Fletcher became the first Democrat and woman to represent the district. Her priorities span energy, healthcare, and gun safety; she has secured nearly $16 million in community project funding for TX-07.
