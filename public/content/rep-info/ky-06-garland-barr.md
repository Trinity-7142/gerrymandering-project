Garland Hale "Andy" Barr IV is an American attorney and politician serving as the U.S. representative for Kentucky's 6th congressional district since 2013. Born and raised in Lexington, Kentucky, his family has deep roots in the city — Barr Street there is reportedly named for one of his ancestors. He earned a B.A. from the University of Virginia and a J.D. from the University of Kentucky College of Law. A conservative Republican, he serves as a senior member of the House Financial Services Committee. Barr is focused on strengthening Kentucky's signature bourbon, equine, coal, agriculture, and manufacturing industries and prioritizes expanding access to affordable health care without government interference between patients and doctors. Barr launched a bid for U.S. Senate in April 2025 to succeed retiring incumbent Mitch McConnell.
